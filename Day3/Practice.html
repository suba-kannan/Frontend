Madurai

Madurai is famous for the Meenakshi Amman Temple, the Gandhi Memorial Museum, and its traditional textiles like Sungudi sarees. The city is also well-known for its unique drink, Jigarthanda, and the vibrant street food, shopping, and markets

Chennai

Chennai is famous for its cultural heritage, including numerous temples and classical arts like Bharatnatyam, and landmarks such as Marina Beach and the ancient Kapaleeshwarar Temple. The city is also a hub for the Tamil film industry, known for its popular cricket team, the Chennai Super Kings, and is the location of India's oldest public zoo and municipal corporation.

Virudhunagar

Virudhunagar is famous for its industries, particularly matchbox manufacturing, fireworks, and printing. It is also a significant market for commodities like edible oils, millet, chicory, coffee, and lentils.

Cudaalore

Cuddalore Tourism: All You Need to Know Before You Go (2025)Cuddalore is famous for its mangrove forests, beaches, and numerous temples, including the Thiruvanthipuram Arulmigu Devanatha Swami Temple and the Veerattaneswarar Temple.

Erode

Erode is famous for its turmeric and textile industry, with the city being a major producer and market for both. Other famous things include several significant temples like the Thindal Murugan Temple and Periya Mariamman Temple, and natural attractions such as the Vellode Bird Sanctuary and Bhavanisagar Dam
